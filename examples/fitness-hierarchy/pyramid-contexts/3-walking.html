Walk 10,000 steps (3 miles, 5 kilometers, 1 hour) every day.

Optimal times to walk are:

  • Upon waking, as part of your morning routine - hitting several goals at once: morning sun, morning activity, walking.
  • After eating a meal. A short, 10-minute walk after a meal has been shown to aid digestion and lower blood sugar.

Use this time to:

  • Meditate: focus on your thoughts and/or emotions and strive to understand them.
  • Goal Setting: reflect on the day and what went well and what could have gone better; consider the upcoming day and what needs to be done.

Implementation (3+ weeks):

  • Walk 1,000 more steps each day until you're averaging 10k steps per day. Continue to do so until you've averaged 10k steps for each of the last 10 days.
