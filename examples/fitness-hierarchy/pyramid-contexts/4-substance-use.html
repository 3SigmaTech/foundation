Reduce or eliminate consumption of nicotine and alcohol. This can most easily be accomplishd by considering the long-form impact of these substances. Do you really feel 100% the next day?

Strategically leverage caffeine - reduce to 200mg (two cups of coffee, four cups of tea) or less per day and use more during short periods (3-4 days) of high output.

Implementation (4+ weeks):

Reduce nicotine, alcohol, and caffeine by one unit per week until you're consuming one unit or less of nicotine or alcohol per week.

  • Nicotine: 10mg (one cigarette) per week or less
  • Alcohol: one drink (16oz beer, 5oz wine, 1.5oz liquor) per week or less
  • Caffeine: 200mg (two cups of coffee, four cups of tea, four diet sodas) per day or less
