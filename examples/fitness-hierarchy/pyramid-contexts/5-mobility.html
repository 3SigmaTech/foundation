Define a baseline level of mobility required for you to perform at the level you want. Be sure to take a holistic overview, including:

  • Hip, knee, ankle mobility
  • Shoulder, elbow, wrist mobility
  • Cervial, thoracic, lumbar spine mobility

Establish a routine to achieve or maintain this level of mobility - daily stretching, weekly yoga, etc.

Implementation (4+ weeks):

  • Perform 15+ minutes of yoga 3x per week for four weeks. Identify areas of weakness and incorporate into your longer form fitness routine to run for 2-3 months. Review and reassess your overall mobility approximately quarterly.
  • This longer form routine could be the same yoga program, or it could be tailored to your individual needs (e.g. tight hamstrings might warrant daily static stretching along with a leg strength program).
